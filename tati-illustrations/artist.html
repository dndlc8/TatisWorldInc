Meet The Artist


After graduating from the UC, Irvine in 2011 with a BA in Drama, Tatiana worked as a freelance artist for film and theater. She designed sets for a variety of theatrical shows and film productions, occasionally working as charge scenic painter and adjunct theater professor at Irvine Valley College. She has worked with professional theater companies like Oregon Shakespeare Festival, Great River Shakespeare Festival, and South Coast Repertory. Additionally, she has worked Art Department alongside Francis Ford Coppola, Michael Wilson and film actors Willem Dafoe and Robert Picardo.
