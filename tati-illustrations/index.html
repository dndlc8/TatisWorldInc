About Tati's World Inc.

I've created another branch to my multifaceted business (film/tv designer and stop motion animation fabricator) called Tati's World Inc. that focuses on cartoon illustrations. Tati's World Inc. is a creative business that shares my passion for creating quirky characters on their silly adventures. The art provided with this business is intended to brighten someone's day for it's creativity and unique perspective of the world. I hope to bring back a bit of fun into people's lives because as we get older, we forget how to play. This art reminds us it's okay to play, even as adults. So please come and play.
